Hi! I am a Ph.D. student at Duke University, where I am fortunate to be supervised by Prof. Neil Gong. My research focuses on security, safety and privacy of generative AI. Priorly, I earned a B.Eng. from the University of Science and Technology of China in 2020. I worked with Prof. Tong Zhang at HKUST in Summer 2019.
