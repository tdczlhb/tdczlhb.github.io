Hi there! I am a third-year Ph.D. student in the Electrical and Computer Engineering department at Duke University, where I am fortunate to be supervised by Prof. Neil Zhenqiang Gong.

Before joining Duke, I received B.Eng. in Information Science from the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in June 2020. I was fortunate to be advised by Prof. Xueyang Fu and Prof. Zheng-Jun Zha during my undergraduate study. I also worked with Prof. Tong Zhang at HKUST in summer 2019.

I am broadly interested in machine learning, security, and privacy. My recent research focuses on trustworthy pre-trained models.
